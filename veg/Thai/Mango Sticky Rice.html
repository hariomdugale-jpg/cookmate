1. Soak the Sticky Rice
Rinse the glutinous rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Transfer to a bowl and cover with cold water. Soak for at least 4 hours or overnight — this step is non-negotiable. Soaking softens the grains so they steam evenly and achieve that signature chewy, sticky texture.
2. Steam the Rice
Drain the soaked rice and spread it in a steamer basket lined with a damp cloth or banana leaf. Steam over boiling water on medium heat for 20–25 minutes until the rice turns translucent and is fully cooked through and sticky. Do not boil it in water — steaming is the only correct method for sticky rice.
3. Make the Sweet Coconut Sauce
While the rice steams, pour the coconut milk into a small saucepan over low heat. Add sugar and a pinch of salt. Stir gently until the sugar fully dissolves — do not boil. The sauce should be smooth, slightly sweet and fragrant. Divide it: two thirds for soaking the rice, one third reserved as a pouring sauce for serving.
4. Soak the Rice in Coconut Sauce
Transfer the freshly steamed hot rice into a bowl. Immediately pour the larger portion of warm coconut sauce over it and stir gently to coat every grain. Cover the bowl with a lid or cling film and let it rest for 15–20 minutes. The rice will absorb all the coconut sauce and become gloriously creamy, rich and fragrant as it sits.
5. Prepare the Mango
Peel the ripe mangoes and slice them into long, clean slices alongside the seed. Alphonso or Kesar mangoes work best for their intense sweetness and deep golden colour. The mango should be perfectly ripe — fragrant, golden and yielding to gentle pressure. Keep refrigerated until serving for a cool contrast to the warm sticky rice.
6. Plate & Serve
Mould a portion of coconut-soaked sticky rice onto one side of a plate using a small bowl or your hands. Fan the mango slices neatly alongside the rice. Drizzle the reserved coconut sauce generously over the rice. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds on top for a gentle nutty crunch. Serve immediately at room temperature or slightly warm for the best experience.
