1. Prepare the Bean Filling
Drain and rinse cooked rajma or black beans. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook for 3–4 minutes until soft. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add the beans and mash roughly — leave some whole for texture. Season with chilli powder, cumin and salt. Cook for 3–4 minutes until the filling is thick and well seasoned.
2. Sauté the Vegetables
In a separate pan heat a little oil. Add sliced bell peppers, sweet corn and a pinch of cumin. Stir-fry on high heat for 3–4 minutes until the vegetables are slightly charred and tender but still have a bite. Season with chilli powder and salt. The charred veg adds a lovely smoky flavour to the tacos.
3. Make the Fresh Salsa
Finely dice tomatoes, onion and green chilli. Mix together with freshly squeezed lime juice, chopped coriander and salt. Stir well and let it rest for 10 minutes so the flavours come together. This fresh pico de gallo salsa brightens up the entire taco with its tangy freshness.
4. Prepare the Guacamole
Scoop the flesh of a ripe avocado into a bowl. Mash with a fork. Add lime juice, finely chopped onion, coriander, green chilli and salt. Mix well. Keep slightly chunky for the best texture. Press plastic wrap directly onto the surface to prevent browning until ready to use.
5. Warm the Taco Shells
For hard shells: place in a preheated oven at 180°C for 3–4 minutes until crispy and warm. For soft tortillas: heat each one directly on a dry tawa or gas flame for 20–30 seconds per side until warm, pliable and lightly charred in spots.
6. Assemble the Tacos
Lay out the warmed taco shells. Start with a spoonful of the bean filling at the base. Add a layer of sautéed peppers and corn. Spoon fresh salsa on top. Add a dollop of guacamole or sour cream. Sprinkle grated cheese generously over everything.
7. Garnish & Serve
Finish with a squeeze of fresh lime juice and a generous scatter of chopped coriander. Serve immediately — tacos are best eaten fresh and crunchy. Serve with extra salsa, sour cream and lime wedges on the side.
